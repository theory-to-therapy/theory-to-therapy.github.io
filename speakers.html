How to Add Your Speaker Profile

To add or update speaker information, edit the speakers.html file:

  1. Copy a speaker card div block
  2. Update the speaker photo path (place images in images/speakers/)
  3. Replace name, affiliation, and bio
  4. Add website and email links

Speaker photos should be square (recommended 300x300px minimum) and will be displayed as circles.
