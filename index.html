About the Conference

The Next Frontier in Medical Data 2026 brings together an interdisciplinary community to address the growing challenges and opportunities in medical data science. As healthcare systems generate unprecedented volumes of complex data, innovative approaches from mathematics, physics, and computational sciences are becoming essential for breakthroughs in diagnosis, treatment, and drug discovery.

Key Topics Include:

  • Mathematical Oncology: Models of tumor growth, evolution, and treatment response
  • Machine Learning for Healthcare: Machine learning/AI for medical imaging, diagnosis, and prognosis
  • Bayesian Methods: Statistical inference for personalised medicine and clinical trials
  • Computational Biology: Systems approaches to understanding disease mechanisms
  • Physics-Informed Modelling: Incorporating physical laws into medical data analysis
  • Clinical Translation: Pathways from theoretical innovation to patient care

This colloquium aims to foster collaboration between theoretical researchers and clinical practitioners, creating new pathways from mathematical innovation to therapeutic impact.
